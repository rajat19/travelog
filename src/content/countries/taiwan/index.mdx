---
title: Taiwan
description: A compact island packed with design-forward cities, night markets, mountain scenery, and a food scene that rewards curiosity at every stop.
---

Taiwan is remarkably easy to love as a travel destination. It combines reliable transport, friendly street-level energy, and a food culture that stretches from carefully brewed tea to loud, crowded night markets where every stall seems to have its own regulars.

The island also gives travelers a lot of variety quickly. In Taipei especially, temples, modern skyline views, historic avenues, and green mountain lookouts all fit into the same compact geography.
