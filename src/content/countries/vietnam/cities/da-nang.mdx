## Where the Mountains Meet the Sea

Da Nang is Vietnam's most livable city, a sparkling coastal gem nestled between the Marble Mountains and the South China Sea. Once a sleepy beach town, it's undergone a remarkable transformation with gleaming bridges, modern infrastructure, and pristine beaches that rival anywhere in Southeast Asia. It makes a perfect base for exploring central Vietnam.

## The Dragon Bridge

Da Nang's Dragon Bridge is a 666-meter engineering marvel shaped like a dragon. On weekend nights, it literally breathes fire and water, a spectacular show that draws thousands of onlookers. The bridge symbolizes the city's ambition and modern spirit. Beyond the bridge, My Khe Beach stretches for miles of pristine white sand, consistently rated among the world's most beautiful beaches.

## Marble Mountains & Beyond

The five Marble Mountains rise dramatically from the coastal plain, each named after one of the five elements. Inside, you'll find Buddhist grottoes, hidden cave temples, and viewpoints overlooking the city and sea. A day trip to the Ba Na Hills takes you to the famous Golden Bridge, held aloft by giant stone hands emerging from the mountainside and creating one of the most photographed spots in Asia.
