## The Manchester of the East

> Kanpur does not cater to tourists. It is a working city with an industrial heart, offering an unfiltered look at urban North India on the banks of the Ganges.

Once known as Cawnpore by the British, Kanpur was a massive textile and leather-producing hub, famously dubbed the "Manchester of the East." Today, it retains its industrial grit. While it lacks the manicured monuments of nearby Lucknow, it holds significant history from the 1857 Indian Mutiny and features some of the country's most prominent universities, including IIT Kanpur.

<Highlight title="Top Kanpur Experiences">
- **Ganga Barrage / Massacre Ghat**: Visiting the banks of the sacred river Ganges at sunset.
- **Blue World Theme Park**: A massive, somewhat chaotic, but highly popular water and amusement park on the edge of the city.
- **J.K. Temple**: A beautiful modern Hindu temple combining ancient North & South Indian architecture.
</Highlight>

<Tip title="Leather Shopping">
Because it is a manufacturing hub, you can buy incredibly high-quality, export-grade leather goods (jackets, shoes, bags) at incredibly cheap prices at the Jajmau leather markets.
</Tip>

## The Food Scene

The street food here is bold, spicy, and heavily traditional. Thaggu Ke Ladoo is a legendary sweet shop that even holds a place in Bollywood pop culture.

<FoodGuide>
| Dish | Where to Try | Price |
|------|--------------|-------|
| **Ladoo** | Thaggu Ke Ladoo | ~₹200/box |
| **Badnaam Kulfi** | Civil Lines | ~₹50 |
| **Kulcha Chole** | Arya Nagar street stalls | ~₹40 |
</FoodGuide>

<Itinerary>
- **Day 1: River and Rhythms** — Morning visit to J.K. Temple → Shop for leather in Jajmau → Catch the sunset and eat Maggi noodles at the Ganga Barrage.
</Itinerary>

## Logistics

<GettingThere>
Kanpur Central is one of the busiest railway stations in the country, on the main Delhi-Kolkata line. Nearly every major train stops here. Lucknow airport is about 1.5 hours away by taxi.
</GettingThere>

<Accommodation title="Where I Stayed">
**Swaroop Nagar / Arya Nagar**: The cleanest, most modern residential and commercial areas in the city, packed with cafes and good street food.
</Accommodation>
