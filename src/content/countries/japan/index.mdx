---
title: Japan
description: Where ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity coexist — from serene zen gardens to electric cityscapes.
---

Japan rewards attention to detail. The beauty is often in the transitions: a quiet shrine approach just off a busy avenue, a train ride that feels perfectly choreographed, or a meal that turns precision into hospitality.

Traveling through different cities here never feels repetitive. Each place sharpens a different side of the country, from hyper-urban pace to ritual, craft, and stillness.
