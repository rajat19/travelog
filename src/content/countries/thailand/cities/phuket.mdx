## Phuket Beyond the Resort Brochure

Phuket can be as lively or as relaxed as you want it to be. Some travelers come for nightlife and beach clubs, others for boat days and slower mornings, and the island is large enough to hold all of those versions at once.

## Bays, Beaches, and Big Views

The scenery is what keeps Phuket in heavy rotation for so many itineraries. Big Buddha sits high above the island, Phang Nga Bay delivers those unmistakable limestone-karst seascapes, and Patong offers a louder, more kinetic beach-town experience.

## The Old Town Surprise

One of Phuket’s nicest contrasts is its old quarter. Colorful Sino-Portuguese buildings, coffee shops, and quieter side streets give the island another identity entirely. It is worth making space for that part of Phuket, not just the coastline.
