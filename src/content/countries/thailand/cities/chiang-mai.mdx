## A Softer Side of Thailand

Chiang Mai trades Bangkok’s scale for something more grounded. Inside the old city walls, temple roofs, low-rise streets, and leafy cafés make it easy to spend whole afternoons wandering without much of an agenda.

## Temples and Mountain Air

The spiritual heart of Chiang Mai is impossible to miss. Wat Phra That Doi Suthep watches over the city from the mountains, while Wat Chedi Luang brings centuries of Lanna history into the center. Even a simple walk through the old quarter feels framed by golden stupas and monastery walls.

## Base for the North

Chiang Mai also works as a gateway. From here, day trips reach waterfalls, national parks, craft villages, and cooler mountain landscapes like Doi Inthanon. The result is a city break that still leaves room for nature.
