## Keep Austin Weird

Austin is Texas's proudly eccentric capital, a city where tech moguls share breakfast tacos with country musicians and "keeping it weird" is a civic duty. The live music capital of the world has a venue on nearly every corner in the entertainment district, from tiny honky-tonks to massive concert halls. The energy is infectious, the people are friendly, and the barbecue is a near-religious experience.

## Music & Nightlife

Sixth Street on a weekend night is a sensory overload, with blues, country, rock, and electronic music pouring from every doorway. The Continental Club has hosted legends since the 1950s. Beyond the bar scene, venues like Stubb's BBQ and the Mohawk host world-class touring acts. During SXSW, the entire city becomes a festival, with surprise performances happening in parking lots, taco joints, and living rooms.

## Nature & Food

Barton Springs Pool is a natural spring-fed swimming pool that stays a refreshing 68 F year-round. Lady Bird Lake offers kayaking and hiking trails through the heart of downtown. The food scene rivals any major city: Franklin Barbecue draws lines at dawn for its legendary brisket, while Torchy's Tacos has elevated the breakfast taco to an art form. The city's food truck culture is thriving, with hundreds of trailers serving everything from Korean-Mexican fusion to gourmet ice cream.
